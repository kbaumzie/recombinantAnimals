Learn How Important Animals are in Biotechnology!

Bioscience has several aspects but genetically modified organisms sure is a popular topic in today's world. One use of this science is engineering to modify living organims, such as animals. This technology helps to improve animals, people, and to develop special microorganisms for specific agricultural uses.

An example of animal biotechnology is creating transgenic animals (animals with one or more genes introduced by human intervention), to make animals with a specific inactivated genes that can help make discoveries based on their interactions with the organism and the environment.
