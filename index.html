Learn How Important Animals are in Biotechnology!

An aspect of bioscience is to engineer and modify living organims, such as animals. This technology helps to improve animals, people, and to develop special microorganisms for specific agricultural uses.

An example of animal biotechnology is creating transgenic animals (animals that have one or more genes introduced by human intervention), to make animals with a specific inactivated genes, reorganized proteins, or modified hormone intake that can help make discoveries based on their interactions with the technology and environment.
